Project Overview

Vision Statement

Project Vision

Music has always brought people together, but finding a space where musicians and enthusiasts can share their perspectives, discover new ideas, and feel genuinely heard is harder than it should be. DISKussMe! exists to change that. Rather than relying on large general-purpose social media platforms where music content can get buried or lost, DISKussMe! focuses entirely on helping users discover and connect through their shared interest in music.
DISKussMe! is a community forum built specifically for music lovers to share their thoughts, experiences, and knowledge about the music that moves them. Whether someone is a seasoned guitarist, a casual listener with strong opinions on genres, or a producer exploring new techniques, DISKussMe! gives them a place to put their voice into the world and connect with others who care just as deeply.
DISKussMe! makes it easy for users to create posts about artists, genres, instruments, performances, albums, or other music topics that matter to them. Users can browse posts, search for content that matches their interests, and explore the profiles of other users to better understand their musical background and perspective. By making it simple to view, create, and revisit music-related content, DISKussMe! aims to encourage users to engage more deeply with music and feel more connected to a community of people with similar interests.
DISKussMe! also supports self-expression by allowing users to personalize their profiles with information about their musical tastes, interests, and instruments they play. They are also able to manage their own content by editing or deleting posts. This helps users present who they are musically, keep their posts relevant and discover others with similar interests.
DISKussMe! will be considered successful when it becomes the go-to space for music-focused discussion. Success will be measured by attracting 500 registered users within the first three months of release, achieving a user retention rate of at least 50% from Month 1 to Month 3, and maintaining an average of 20 or more new posts per week sustained over that same period.

Intended Users

Target Audience

DISKussMe! is a music-focused app that empowers music lovers, from musicians to casual listeners, who want to share their perspectives, discover new ideas, and connect with others who share their passion for music.

Design

Design Smells or Strong Design Decisions

Early in iteration 2, the team decided to refactor the UI into a fragments-based architecture, which improved modularity and maintainability.

Tech Debt

Outstanding Bugs or Technical Debt

A piece of technical debt that did not get resolved is the ability to create an account on the DiskussMe platform. In iterations 2 & 3, we created the ability for users to login and logout of the application, better separating the current user from others. However, if a new user wanted to join the platform, they have no way to register an account and will simply be met with a login page they can’t complete.
The technical debt is deliberate and prudent. The ability to add new users is out of the scope of our current project goals. Specifically, this is an implementation detail we will “deal with later” prior to an official public release.
Given one more iteration, to implement new user registration, we would need the following in each layer.
Presentation: A button located on the login page linked to a new fragment that allows the user to create a unique username and password. Other profile features such as a picture, tags and description would be available to edit after account creation in the profile tab.
Logic: A set of functions to ensure the user does not already exist. That is, the username is unique and the password meets certain expectations (longer then 5 characters, contains a number, etc.)
Persistence: The ability to store a new user.
